F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

What is FigLinks?

FigLinks is an AI-powered platform that conducts automated interviews and provides candidate scores to help businesses streamline their hiring process.

How do AI interviews work?

Our AI agents conduct structured interviews, analyze candidate responses, and generate objective scores based on predefined criteria, helping you make data-driven hiring decisions.

Can the AI assess both technical and soft skills?

Yes, our AI evaluates both technical expertise and soft skills using advanced NLP, voice analysis, and behavioral insights.

Is the AI agent customizable?

Absolutely! You can tailor interview questions, scoring criteria, and assessment parameters to fit your hiring needs.

Does this replace human recruiters?

No, our platform enhances recruiter efficiency by automating initial interviews and providing insights, allowing HR teams to focus on final decision-making.

How do I get started?

Simply sign up, set up your interview parameters, and let our AI handle the rest. Contact us for a demo or free trial!
